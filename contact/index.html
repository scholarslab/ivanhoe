Credits

The present version of Ivanhoe is being developed and maintained by the Praxis Program. We’re a cohort of graduate students brought together by the University of Virginia Scholars’ Lab to build digital skills and prepare for emergent academic and alt-ac careers. This year’s cohort (2014–15) is the second to work on Ivanhoe, and the fourth overall—see our sister program Prism for the work carried out by the first two cohorts. The Praxis Program gratefully acknowledges the support of the following groups:
  • The Praxis Network
  • The University of Virginia
  • The Scholars' Lab

Contact
