Installing the Ivanhoe WP Theme

To install Ivanhoe, you will first need a WordPress page. To get started using WordPress, check out its documentation at WordPress Codex. Once you have your own WordPress set up, click the button below to download our Ivanhoe Game WP Theme.

[Download button here]

Once you have installed the Ivanhoe Game Theme, go to your http://localhost/wordpress/ page. Click on "Themes," and then select your newly downloaded Ivanhoe Game Theme. This will apply the theme to your WP page. You will then be ready to play Ivanhoe.

As Praxis operates in the spirit of open-source development, we have stored all our files and source code on GitHub. Feel free to take a look, see what makes Ivanhoe work, and contribute your ideas for its improvement.
